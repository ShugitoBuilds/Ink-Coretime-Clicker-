## 1. Information We Collect

### Blockchain Data
- Public wallet addresses (visible on blockchain)
- Transaction history (public on blockchain)
- Contract interactions (public on blockchain)

### Application Data
- No personal information collected
- No cookies or tracking (unless explicitly stated)
- No user accounts or registration

## 2. How We Use Information

- **Blockchain Data**: Used to process transactions and determine winners
- **No Personal Data**: We do not collect, store, or process personal data
- **No Third-Party Sharing**: We do not share data with third parties

## 3. Data Storage

- **On-Chain**: Transaction data is stored on Kusama blockchain (public)
- **No Off-Chain Storage**: We do not store user data off-chain
- **Wallet Extension**: Wallet data managed by Polkadot.js extension (not by us)

## 4. Third-Party Services

- **Polkadot.js Extension**: Wallet management (subject to their privacy policy)
- **RPC Providers**: Blockchain data access (subject to their privacy policy)
- **Hosting Providers**: Frontend hosting (subject to their privacy policy)

## 5. Your Rights

- **Access**: All blockchain data is publicly accessible
- **Deletion**: Blockchain data cannot be deleted (immutable)
- **Correction**: Contact us if you believe data is incorrect

## 6. Security

- We implement security measures to protect the service
- However, blockchain transactions are public and irreversible
- You are responsible for securing your wallet and private keys

## 7. Children's Privacy

- Service is not intended for users under 18
- We do not knowingly collect data from children
